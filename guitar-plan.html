2-Month Electric Guitar Roadmap

The 9-5 Practice Block

  • 00-05m: Tuning (Crucial for ear training).
  • 05-10m: Spider Walk (Fret 5-8 warm-up).
  • 10-20m: The "Core Drill" (1-min chord changes).
  • 20-30m: The Song (Reward yourself).

Phase 1: Foundations (Weeks 1-2)

Learn A, D, and E Major. Master the "Air Change" (hands off/on neck).

Phase 2: Rhythm (Weeks 3-4)

Learn G and C Major. Focus on the "D-D-U-D-U" Folk Strum.

Phase 3: Mood & Minors (Weeks 5-6)

Learn Am and Em. Start Hozier's Work Song.

Phase 4: Full Mastery (Weeks 7-8)

The Stable Song. Record yourself and check for dead notes.
