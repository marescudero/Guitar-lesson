Phase 1: Foundations (Wks 1-2)

A, D, E Major shapes. Focus on thumb position and arching fingers.

Phase 2: Rhythm Engine (Wks 3-4)

G & C Major. Practice the "Folk Strum" (D-D-U-D-U) on muted strings.

Phase 3: Dark Folk (Wks 5-6)

Am & Em. Begin Hozier's Work Song. Focus on palm muting.

Phase 4: Performance (Wks 7-8)

The Stable Song. Practice with YouTube set to 0.75x speed.

Electric Guitar Tips

  • Pickup: Switch to "Neck" (Top Position).
  • Reverb: Set to level 4 for that airy indie vibe.
  • Pressure: Electric strings are soft. Press just enough to ring.

Teacher's Wisdom

Desk Practice: Keep a stand by your 9-5 desk. Play 3 chords every time you finish a task.

Anchor Fingers: When moving G to C, keep your shape consistent as you shift strings.
